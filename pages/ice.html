Ice Cream Goat

On a cut and have access to a (good) blender/processor? Fan of feeling full? Then this is the one for you.

Ingredients:

  • Protein Powder - 45g
  • Cocoa Powder - 5-10g
  • Xanthan Gum - 5g ish
  • Frozen (Staw/Blue/Rasp)berries - 100g
  • Ice - 100g
  • Water - 100ml

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together first, ideally.

  2. Blend the berries and ice, so they're crushed.

  3. Add the dry ingredients to the frozen blend, add the water and mix.
