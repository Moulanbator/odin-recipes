The Classic

They don't call this The Classic for nothing! This recipe is a staple of any dieatry regime,

and quite simple to recreate. Let me show you how!

Ingredients list for 5 servings:

  • Chicken - 2kg
  • Rice - 250g
  • Broccoli - 1kg
  • Seasonings

Instructions:

  1. Marinate yo chicken - go crazy. Let it soak up da juices. Personally:

    Soy Sauce, Salt, Pepper, Meat Seasoning.

  2. Chuck the chicken in the oven, blast the heat, like 20-25mins.

  3. Wash your damn rice. Cook it however you cook rice.

  4. Wash and cut up your broccoli. Steam 'till aldente.

  5. Serve into 5 tupperwares equally, refrigerate.
