Diabolical Brownies

Unfortunately, they will most likely not look (or taste) as good as they do in this picture.

But for the kcals they have, it's almost worth it.

Ingredients for 1 batch, 567kcals:

  • Cocoa Powder - 64g
  • Protein Powder - 36g
  • Erythritol - 135g
  • Baking Powder - 1/2 tspn
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Diet Coke - 360g

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven @ 190C.

  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.

  3. Mix the coke into your dry ingredients.

  4. Pour mix into baking tray, which you obviously covered with a baking sheet.

  5. Chuck in the oven for a while, in theory 30mins - has taken up to 1hr.
